How was Fintech adopted in Portugal?

Portugal Fintech is a startup-focused network that brings together key industry stakeholders – from founders, investors, regulators, and academia to incumbents in banking, insurance, consulting, and law firms – to foster the growth of the Fintech ecosystem. The main activities aim to connect entrepreneurs with clients, give them access to investors, and assist them in navigating regulatory systems. As a result, the purpose is driven by a close engagement with and connection to the ecosystem, providing a common ground for discourse and fostering sustainable Fintech growth. Four pillars of action support the activity: talent and academia, capital and investors, partners and clients, and regulation and policy.
