How was Fintech adopted in Spain?

In terms of adoption index, Spain is ranked 21st globally and 7th in Europe. Increased participation of the Spanish banking industry in fintech, both in terms of capital and building incubation/acceleration programs, with new collaborative ties or competition, is one of the causes for the proliferation of FinTechs in Spain. Fintechs' agility in adopting new technology and testing new business models, as well as their proximity to today's and tomorrow's clients, contribute to these collaborations. Unlike traditional banking, Spanish fintechs are not generic solutions; they can focus primarily on fixing a specific problem for their users. This enables them to grasp users' requirements quickly.
