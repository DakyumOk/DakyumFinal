What is Fintech?

Fintech is the merge of finance and technology. It is the catch-all term referring to software mobile applications, and other technologies created to improve and automate traditional forms of finance for businesses and consumers alike. Fintech includes mobile payment apps to complex blockchain networks housing encrypted transactions. There are two most relevant segments being Payment services and leading and solutions.

A common example of Fintech in our society would be Apple pay. Apple users are often at ease when they forget their wallet at home since the accessibility to their bank accounts are available through a touch pay system within their phone. As consumer demands change, many businesses adopt the ability for consumers to utilize Apply Pay.
